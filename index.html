Population, Politics, and the COVID Vaccine

According to this Bloomberg article, political party affiliation has a strong correlation with vaccination rates across metro areas. In other words, metro areas with higher vaccination rates are more likely to skew Democratic in the polls.

This isn't always the case. There are places like Rosebud County, MT, where 76.2% of the population is fully vaxxed despite the polls going red in the 2020 election. There are also places like Fulton County, GA, where the Democratic vote won majority but 30.5% of the population is fully vaxxed. There are many other counties similar to these.
